ABOUT

Altri is a smart divider pad and app system that improves user productivity through timing and external cues, while minimizing distractions by separating the user from outer distractions. Altri transforms into its space, acting as a work pad or partition, using visual cues to notify the user when they are being distracted from their work or when they need to take a break.

PROBLEM

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic 42% of the US labor force is now working remotely causing workspace and flow to drastically change. Working from home has become the new normal, causing remote workers to struggle with the mental separation of work and play, due to the inability to physically split small spaces within the home. In turn these workers have faced major struggles in maintaining productivity.

SOLUTION

Altri is a multi-functional workspace tool, intended to help users stay productive by physically splitting up leisure and work spaces within various environments. Altri helps create an efficient workflow with its supplementary app that personalizes to each users work flow needs and goals. By creating an efficient workflow and a set space to work, users are able to work more productively in shorter increments, creating much needed free time and separation from work.

Privacy

The Altri fold provides a physical barrier between the user and outside environmental stimuli, creating a sense of privacy and personal space

Comfort

Altri provides freedom for the user to work in a larger, more spacious, and possibly public area without distraction. Workers value large spacious areas while working but often times do not have private access to such space.

Focus

Promotes focus through the use of external cue that let the users know when they have reached their goal and it is time for a break. This prevents burnout and overworking that affects not only the users work but also mental health.

Mobility

Foldable for easy portability and set up in any envrionment. The altri fold is equiped with magnetic inner boards to hold paper and other work materials to provide even more ease during transportation.

WHY ALTRI?

The US labor force, is and has been experiencing a shift towards remote and work from home working, making at home productivity more important than ever. Altri is different from other productivity trackers because it adjusts itself to the user’s workflow, helps communicate a user’s productivity to them through interactive information visuals, and helps separate the distracting aspects of one's home from them while they work. With 42% of the US labor force now working from home due to the pandemic it is imperative to to find a productive workflow that works for you.

Partner App

The Altri app system is a key component to achieving the most with your smart divider pad. The app serves as the main hub for customization of external cues, lights and goals. The Altri app is where the user can compare and anlyize their producitivty levels by day, week, month, and all time.

On Boarding

Welcome screens introduce new users to Altri, its key functions, and a basic understanding of the key elements of both the pad and app. These on boarding screens are also used for users to input personal information for the smart pad and app to use.

Productivity Tracking

The Altri app recives information from both the pad and outside applications to log and categorize your personal produtivity and compares it to the time spent on other areas. Users can compare productivity by day, week, month and all time.

Altri Personalization

The altri features fully comtomizable cues, lights, and goals. The app can be used to edit current defult cues or add new ones. Users can also customize light color and temperature for optimal comfort.

User Interface

The idea for the Altri interface was to design something that aligned with our 3-dimensional theme of mimicking real-world counterparts of in-store shopping in both how they appear and how the user interacts with them. To convey this feeling for the user we created a Skeuomorphism / Neomorphism interface. Skeuomorphism makes the interface objects familiar to users mental models by using concepts they recognize.

Branding

The aim for Sift’s branding is to keep it clean and fun. Typeface Open Sans and poppins both reflect the idea of Sift by providing a consistent, legible, and friendly typographic voice. The colors remain accessible and friendly promoting enjoyment and the efficiency of the fashion hunt.
