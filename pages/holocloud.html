Concept

Holo-cloud is a data management and security product that allows users to visualize the entirety of their personal assets and control what they would like to protect most by utilizing RAID configuration technology.Holo-cloud create an easy and convenient data security solution that helps users manage their data, but also informs and educates users to value their sensitive information more.

Problem

With the increasing complexity of technology, it becomes difficult for users to keep track of their information. They lack knowledge and resources on data security, and at times, the level of threat is underestimated.

These factors cause stagnation in the management of information and development of complacency.

Solution

Holo-cloud is a data management and security product that allows users to visualize and understand the entirety of their personal data assets and control and protect that which is important.

Project Process

We are currently in the development phase of our project.

We are currently in the second half of our senior project. At this phase in our project we are working on developing a working hologram using leap motion. We will then test our hologram with users and evolve our product for the final delivery.
