HI, I'M TAYLOR!

I am a Chicago and Savannah based Visual & UX/UI Designer. Currently, I am a User Experience design major and Graphic Design minor at the Savannah College of Art and Design. I will be graduating in the spring of 2021.


My passions focus heavily on human-centered design. With every project my goal is to create solutions that help people. When I'm not working, I am typically spending time in the gym, searching for the next best coffee shop, or watching cute dog videos.


My interests also lie in fashion marketing, motion media, and fast-changing technologies and programs.
